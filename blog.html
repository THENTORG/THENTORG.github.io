PV Robotics Camp
Tristan 25 June, 2014

After due consideration, it has been decided that we will post a link to the Pine View Robotics Summer Camp on our home page for easy access. Having volunteered and participated in the camp myself, I am pleased to be able to help. The camp is expertly run and a very fun experience for children of all ages and skill levels. Please feel free to head over to the pvrobotics website for more information. Unfortunately, the camp is limited to 40 campers per week, so the camp tends to fill up very quickly.
