Pine View Robotics Camp

The right way for children to get into robotics.

Teachers and student volunteers from Pine View School are once again offering their robotics camp. The camp involves children (rising from grades 5th through 9th) working in small groups to create and program a robot using the LEGO MINDSTORMS Robotics Kit. At the end of camp there will be a little competition to see who was paying attention. We at THENTORG have participated and volunteered at this camp and have been very pleased with how the camp is run. Sign up today for an experience your child will not forget.
